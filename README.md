# Decision DNA Tracker 🧬
### *The Cognitive Backbone for High-Leverage Product Teams*

**Decision DNA Tracker** is more than just a log—it is a system for capturing, auditing, and scaling your team's collective intelligence. In fast-moving organizations, the *why* behind a decision is often more valuable than the *what*. When that context is lost to Slack threads or employee churn, teams suffer from "Decision Amnesia," leading to repetitive debates, unvalidated assumptions, and strategic drift.

This tool turns transient conversations into structured, searchable, and evaluative institutional memory.

---

## 🎯 The Problem: "Decision Amnesia"
Every organization suffers from three critical knowledge leaks:
1.  **The Context Void:** A year from now, no one remembers why we chose *Vendor A* over *Vendor B*, or why we implemented *Feature X* with specific constraints.
2.  **Zombie Proposals:** Bad ideas that were rightfully rejected 6 months ago are proposed again because the original rejection logic wasn't documented.
3.  **The Feedback Gap:** Teams launch features and move to the next "big thing" without ever looking back to see if their initial hypothesis was actually correct.

## 💡 The Solution: A Living DNA Map
Decision DNA Tracker enforces a rigorous but lightweight framework for the entire decision lifecycle:

### 1. High-Fidelity Capture (The Entry)
Instead of vague notes, every decision is logged with its **Trigger**, **Context**, and **Problem Statement**. We don't just record the winner; we record the **Options Considered** with their respective Pros and Cons.

### 2. Hypothesis-Driven Thinking
We force teams to state a clear **Hypothesis**: *"If we do X, we expect Y outcome by Z date."* This transforms "vibes-based" decisions into measurable bets.

### 3. The Feedback Loop (The Evaluation)
Using automated **Review Reminders**, the tool prompts owners to return to past decisions and log the **Actual Outcome**. 
- Was the hypothesis validated? 
- Did we fail for the reasons we expected?
- What did we learn?

### 4. Relational Intelligence (The Graph)
Decisions aren't isolated events. They are nodes in a chain. Our **Decision Graph** visualizes how one strategic choice branched into five tactical executions, allowing new hires to trace the "DNA" of a product back to its first principles.

### 5. The Graveyard (Learning from Rejection)
We archive "Dead Ideas." Before proposing a major shift, stakeholders can search the Graveyard to see if that path was already explored and why it was deemed non-viable.

---

## 🛠 Strategic Impact
- **Onboarding Velocity:** New leads can understand the history of the codebase and product strategy in days, not months.
- **Improved Decision Quality:** The simple act of documenting options and pros/cons reduces cognitive bias.
- **Knowledge Persistence:** Protect your company from the "Context Tax" paid when key team members leave.

---

## 🚀 Technical Architecture (For the Engineers)
Built for speed, reliability, and security:
- **Next.js 15 (App Router)** & **TypeScript** for a rock-solid front-end.
- **PostgreSQL (Neon)** with **Drizzle ORM** for structured data integrity.
- **NextAuth.js v5** for secure, multi-tenant workspace isolation.
- **D3.js** for interactive, complex relationship mapping.
- **Resend** for automated lifecycle triggers and evaluation loops.
